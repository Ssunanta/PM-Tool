What is a Project Manager?

A Project Manager (PM) is the cornerstone of project success, responsible for planning, executing, and delivering projects on time and within budget while managing team dynamics and stakeholder expectations.

Planning & Strategy

  • Define project scope and objectives
  • Create detailed project timelines
  • Identify resources and budget needs
  • Develop risk mitigation strategies

Team Leadership

  • Build and manage project teams
  • Facilitate effective communication
  • Motivate and support team members
  • Resolve conflicts professionally

Execution & Monitoring

  • Track project progress and milestones
  • Monitor budget and resources
  • Manage project changes and scope
  • Address issues and obstacles

Stakeholder Management

  • Maintain stakeholder engagement
  • Provide regular status updates
  • Manage expectations
  • Ensure deliverable quality

Essential PM Tools & Practices

Daily Responsibilities

  • Check task status and blockers
  • Communicate with team members
  • Update project documentation
  • Schedule and conduct meetings
  • Track metrics and KPIs

Weekly Activities

  • Review project progress
  • Plan upcoming sprints/phases
  • Address escalations
  • Update stakeholders
  • Team performance reviews
